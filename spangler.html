Farewell to an amazing physician

At OBGYN Associates, we are grateful for the opportunity to play a role in the lives of our patients and to be a part of their personal stories. We understand that one chapter in each storymust end to begin another. So it is with mixed emotions that we announce Dr. David Spangler’s closing of a chapter here with us.

As of August 1, 2018, Dr. Spangler will be retiring from his practice at OBGYN Associates. While we are sure you wish Dr. Spangler well, you might be wondering how and where you’ll continue your care.

That's why we’d like to extend an invitation to have you continue with one of our practice physicians.
We know you’ll likely have questions as you make decisions about your personal care. Please feel free to give us a call at our Madison or Decatur office for answers to your questions or to schedule an appointment with one of our physicians.
